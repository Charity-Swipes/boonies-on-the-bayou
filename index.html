Our Story

Born from a Pop-Up, Built for the Bayou

What started as Boonies BBQ & Catfish — a beloved pop-up at Trackside Bar in Pearl River — has grown into a full brick-and-mortar destination on the waterfront of Bay St. Louis.

We slow-smoke our meats over real wood, make our pastas and sides from scratch, and source Gulf seafood as fresh as the bayou breeze. Whether you're here for the half rack of ribs, a seafood platter, or our famous smoked wings — you'll leave full, happy, and planning your next visit.
